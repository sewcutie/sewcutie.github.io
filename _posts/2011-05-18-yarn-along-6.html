Joining in with Ginny at Small Things yarn along this week.

I cast on a new project this week.  I decided to go ahead and make Scarlett a dress from a pattern I shared in a previous yarn along.  At the time I'd purchased acrylic yarn suggested in the pattern and as much as I loved the colour, I didn't like the feel of the acrylic yarn.  I purchased some yarn this week that was 100% wool and only $3 a ball.  It's a very similar colour to what I was going to use.  I was also able to return the acrylic yarn that I had originally purchased.  The yarn appears quite blue in my photo, but I would describe it as more like teal (not great light here today for photos).
