Greg and I were married at Proudfoots on the River, Warrnambool, Victoria.  The ceremony was held in a converted boatshed (very quaint actually).  It rained, of course, but once the vows were all said and done, the rain stopped.  It was a wonderful day that was filled with many special memories and people.
I love Greg dearly, more as each year passes and I am so glad I have him by my side as we journey through the years.  We have three beautiful kids, a home, mortgage, well, the whole package really.  Wouldn't have it any other way.  We only hope the next 10 years can be just as much fun, filled with all the wonder of new adventures waiting to be had.  Love you sweetheart, Jacinta xx
