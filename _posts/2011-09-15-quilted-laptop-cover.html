I am so excited to have finally made my trusty Mac laptop a cover.  I was determined not to go out and buy a cover and make my own.  It only took most of this year to get to it.  Of course it took no time to make once I started.  I made the pattern up as I went.

I made it out of a honey bun from Sweetwater's Make Life fabric range.  Oh, I do love me some pre-cuts.  The grey and white spotted lining fabric I purchase at Spotlight.  I'm really happy with how it has sewn up and hope I can avoid scratching my laptop any further.  The cover also acts as a mat to sit under my laptop too.
