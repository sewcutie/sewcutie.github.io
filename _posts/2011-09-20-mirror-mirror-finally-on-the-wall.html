Now these renovations of ours are going very slowly.  But I am happy to say Greg finally got around to hanging a mirror in our diningroom.  The mirror waited very patiently under our bed (out of reach of busy little hands) for many months.  I love how it has added a bit of life to the bricks of our chimney.  Sometimes it is the little jobs that make all the difference.  Jacinta x
