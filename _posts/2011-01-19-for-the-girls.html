I received some fabric goodness in the mail today.  I decided that I would like to make a quilt each for the girls for their birthdays.  I had a lovely time choosing out what I wanted from the Fat Quarter Shop.  I am definitely buying from them again.  Great communication and prompt shipment.  I chose the new Sunkissed fabric (By Sweetwater) for Tenneille's quilt and the pattern is called Summer Breeze.  I love the bunting on the quilt.  For Scarlett I chose a design by Amy Hamberlin called Marabella.  It came in a kit form.  I ordered Anna Maria's Innocent Crush charm pack for myself.  Not sure what I am going to make with it yet.  Let's just say I am one happy woman with big ambitions.  With birthday deadlines to meet, I hope to have my girls snuggled under a whole lot of handmade loveliness in the not too distant future.
