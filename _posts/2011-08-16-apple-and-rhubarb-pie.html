I spied rhubarb in the fruit and veg shop the other day and had an instant craving for apple and rhubarb pie.  This is a simple, yet fulfilling dessert that is enjoyed by everyone in this family.  My recipe doesn't have cane sugar.  I use honey as a sweetner and there is no sugar in the crust.  I hope you enjoy it as much as we do.

Ingredients:

2 cups plain flour
120gm butter
pinch salt
2-3 tbspn (approximately) water

500gm rhubarb
1.5kg apples (I use about 1kg Granny Smith apples & 500gm of a sweeter apple like Royal Gala)
1/2 cup honey
