I want to share with you one of my favourite cafes in the Dandenong Ranges.  Ripe Cafe is a place I found a few years ago in Sassafras.  They make the best baguettes (I couldn't wait to take a photo before biting into mine!) and their coffee is pretty good too.  If you love a hot chocolate made with real melted chocolate sitting at the bottom of your cup, you will love this place.  Meals are available too.  Cakes always look divine.  I must admit that I have only tried a couple of cakes, one being a flourless chocolate cake and an apple pie (not both at once of course!).   They were delicious.

Definitely a place worthy of a visit.  Just a tip, it can get pretty busy on a weekend.  Sometimes it pays to book ahead.
