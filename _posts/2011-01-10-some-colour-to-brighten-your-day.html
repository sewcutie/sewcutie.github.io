Most of the plants on our block are natives.  We have a scattering of azaleas and rododendrons too.  We also have two hydrangea plants just outside of the girls bedroom.  They are at their best right now and the colours just about take my breath away.  The hues of the flowers range from white to blue to mauve.  I love how they change colour on a daily basis.  Enjoy!
