Oh my goodness.  This is one of these toppings for pancakes or crepes that makes my mouth water.  I haven't shared this one before, but I feel it is blog worthy!

Ingredients -

2 bananas - chopped into 1cm pieces
butter
brown sugar
cream
dark rum - optional (but this is where the flavour lies) or non alcoholic substitute
