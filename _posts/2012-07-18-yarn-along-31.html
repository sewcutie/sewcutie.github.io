I have put aside my Beyond Puerperium from last week to finish for Jairah at a later date.  I have cast on another in a smaller size.  It does seem to be a pattern that runs on the large side.  I know my gauge is right.  I am knitting the 0-3 month size and it looks closer to 6 months, which should be fine for my friend's baby, as I am aiming for it to fit for the coming European winter.  I'm using Bendigo Woollen Mills luxury 8ply in Lake.  It's a lovely grey/blue and buttery soft.
