The Dandenong Ranges are filled with beautiful places to visit.  One of which is the National Rhododendron Gardens in Olinda.  As of the middle of this year, entry is now free!  We visit on a regular basis as the gardens are forever changing with the seasons.  We recently had grandparents visit from New Zealand and we managed to pick the ONE sunny day to visit the gardens.
Enjoy your look around.
