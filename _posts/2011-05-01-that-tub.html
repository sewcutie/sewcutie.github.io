I love how simple items around the house have many varied uses for children.  That item at the moment is a plastic tub.  Jairah uses it as his bath tub, but it also serves as a comfy place to read books or to play with trains.  He thinks it's wonderful if he gets pushed around in it too!  The tub gets a good work out by the girls as well, and it can become quite the fought over wanted item in the playroom.

Of course he loves the car, but obviously that isn't the safest place for him to play.

I love how my children find fun in simple ways that keep them entertained for long periods of time.  What items do you find your children use to entertain themselves?
