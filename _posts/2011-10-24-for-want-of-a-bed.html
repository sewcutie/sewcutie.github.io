Cardamon was in need of a new doggy bed.  The one he had had seen better days and we were constantly tripping over it.  So off I went with the kids to see what we could find.  It seemed everything we looked at was expensive and not very well made.  I had an idea and whipped the kids off with me to Kmart.  One basket, one pillow and one pillow case later, I think he has a pretty special bed.  He is currently curled up in his new basket sound asleep. That is proof enough for me that he likes his new digs.

 

As for the cost:

1 basket $14
1 pillow $4
1 pillowcase $5
Total $23
