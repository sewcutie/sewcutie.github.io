Tenneille's bedroom is in the throws of being renovated.  The girls have been sharing a room, but we have decided they could do with their own room and help spread the mess have space to play.  The floating floor is in progress, the walls and wardrobe are painted.  I just need to get a block of time to sew curtains.  I am still deciding if I want to do roman blinds or just the regular pleated curtains.  I chose the fabric pictured below from Sweetwater's Sunkissed secret garden range, printed by Moda.  It is the lemonade colour way.

[caption id="attachment_789" align="aligncenter" width="485" caption="The yellow is actually a little lighter than depicted in photo. "][/caption]

It matches the feature wall we have painted beautifully.  I ordered the fabric from 1choice4quilting.  Their Sunkissed fabric is a good price and I needed to buy quite a lot.  Shipping price to Australia was very reasonable.  Shawna from 1choice4quilting threw in a few extras too.  I love the saying on the compact mirror/mini sewing kit.  It is definitely how I feel about housework!

I can't wait to show you the room once it is complete.  Jacintax
